5 Ways to Stay Active This Winter

Central Texas winters are mild, but shorter days can make it tempting to stay indoors. Try morning walks at Garey Park, join a weekday pickleball group, or check out the fitness classes at your local rec center. Many WilCo gyms and studios offer senior discounts on monthly memberships.
