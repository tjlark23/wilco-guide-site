Business Response

We take guest feedback seriously; however, some of the claims in this review do not align with our operations, including factual inaccuracies about ownership and how our service is structured, which implies there could be a different motive for this review. On New Year’s Day, the restaurant was in fact very busy and a host was staffed at the door throughout service, making a prolonged wait to be greeted unlikely. Our kitchen does not hold food under heat lamps for extended periods (impossible to be honest, given the actual space of the area and the need to move items off the holding area to make room for new ones), and our payment system does not allow guests to be charged on a credit card that has already declined without presenting that same payment method for reprocessing. The combination of concerns raised, including reports of unacceptable service alongside a claimed 100% tip, does create some confusion around the details of this account. We’re always willing to review legitimate concerns and welcome the opportunity to do so directly. If this visit occurred as described, we invite the reviewer to contact us at perkybeanscoffee@yahoo.com with the payment details so we can look into the matter further. We stand by our team and our commitment to operating with integrity and transparency.
