★★★★★

There are a few Sip Saam Thai locations. We gave this one a try. Hawaiian theme and all. Our food was good. Mine had a few cold spots where it may not have been stirred well enough during cooking. But again, it was good. The bench seating is hard. No cushions. Lemongrass has cushions (finally) on its benches, fyi. The Service is excellent. Not so busy on a Saturday night around 7:30 pm. Another thing was the music choices. I was expecting maybe Thailand or Hawaii themed relaxing music to get into the Vibe of the menu. Nope. Country music and it just didn’t match the ambience. So if you like eating Thai food, on a hard bench, with Country music playing. Come here. But with my back, I will stick with lemongrass and the padded cushions on the benches. Your call.
