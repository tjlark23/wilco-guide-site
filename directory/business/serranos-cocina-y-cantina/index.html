My family has been eating at Serrano’s for over 20 years. We’ve visited different locations over the years, but they all offer the same great menu and consistently good service. I really appreciate how wheelchair accessible they are, with parking and plenty of room to get around inside too. The decor is bright and fun, and the restaurant is always clean. They offer a full bar with great drinks, and the chips and salsa are terrific. My favorite dish is the pollo perfecto, and the portions are very generous. Serrano’s is family friendly and has a children’s menu as well. If you haven’t been yet, give them a try. You won’t be disappointed! Side note- Iforgot to take pictures of the food because once they brought it to the table all thoughts left my head and we just dug in!
