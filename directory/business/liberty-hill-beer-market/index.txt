Liberty Hill Beer Market checks every box. From the moment you walk in, the vibe is comfortable, welcoming, and full of great energy. It has that perfect neighborhood feel with an awesome group of regulars and a staff that truly makes you feel at home.
The food was excellent. I had the sausage platter and risotto on my first visit—both were outstanding—and I enjoyed it so much I went back the very next day for lunch. The chili was incredible. Clearly a place that takes pride in its menu, not just the beer.
