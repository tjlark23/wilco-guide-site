Agnes Sullivan
1mo ago
★★☆☆☆

Went here for lunch today and unfortunately it was very underwhelming compared to how this location used to be. The restaurant itself looks run down. The bathrooms were in rough shape. The sink had visible, permanent stains, and there was an electrical outlet installed directly underneath the soap dispenser, which doesn’t look like something that would ever pass inspection. When I approached the counter to order, I wasn’t greeted at all. The employee just stared at me and didn’t say a word. I had to initiate the interaction myself and place my order. She handed me my receipt and buzzer without a thank you or any acknowledgment. It was uncomfortable and unwelcoming. The gentleman who brought my food out was polite, which was a bright spot. I later noticed I was charged twice. I asked the bartender, Elizabeth, about it. After speaking with the manager, they told me one charge was pending and the duplicate should fall off in 24 to 48 hours. I kept my receipt, but there was no apology, just a very “that’s how it is” attitude. Overall, the energy felt off. Everyone seemed like they didn’t want to be there. As for the food, the fried avocado taco had brown avocado when I bit into it. The shrimp taco was just okay, and both flour tortillas were definitely stale. This place used to be fun and reliable, which makes this experience disappointing. I really hope they can get this location back to where it once was, because right now it honestly felt worse than a Taco Bell.
