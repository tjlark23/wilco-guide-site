Ethan, Thanks for noticing our team’s spirit and admiring our restaurant. Thank you for also sharing your thoughts on our payment options; we hear you. We made the decision to go cashless for the safety of our team, as we didn’t want to have large amounts of cash on hand as our location isn’t big enough to carry any safes. We also didn’t want to worry about money drops. We have found that going cashless has made for smoother transactions all the way through. We do also offer Venmo and Cashapp as a form of payment aside from credit cards. We are sorry that we didn’t meet your needs as far as cash is concerned, but we hope that you understand. 💜, The Thirsty Chicken 🐓
