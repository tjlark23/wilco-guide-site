HighPointe Estate was absolutely the right choice for our wedding, and we’re so grateful to Dawson for bringing our vision to life! We had a full wedding weekend with both Indian and American traditions, and HPE gave us all the flexibility we were looking for across multiple days, guest counts, and decor. First, there’s the property itself. Beautiful, big spaces with the ability to flex for a more intimate guest count. There’s intention in every detail, down to the restrooms. The chapel is so sweet and the big windows make for gorgeous photos. The grounds are well-maintained and were the perfect backdrop for our portraits. The real magic of HPE though is DAWSON. He is so fun to work with, has a clear passion for what he does, and has an incredible eye for color, shape, and floral design! In Dawson, I had a friend with a creative energy that matched my own—and he’s willing to flex, think, and try new things while maintaining flawless execution and being mindful of your budget. I wanted our “American Day” to feel different than the “Indian Day” for our guests even though both were at HighPointe, with a request to reuse as many flowers as we could between days while introducing a few new colors on the second day to mix things up. We also brainstormed on how to create a “mandap” structure for the Indian day and landed on the idea of ditching the poles for a standing structure to hang the installation from the rafters instead. IT WAS BREATHTAKING. True perfection. I’ll let the photos speak for themselves. Finally, to simplify things we chose to use the full-service package for our American Day to simplify the planning, and that was so worth it. Dawson has done a great job curating the vendors he works with, and it was through the package that we were introduced to the wedding planner of our dreams: Lauren Morgan with Day of Lily. THANK YOU, THANK YOU to Dawson and the HPE team for being so much more than the backdrop for our wedding weekend. We remember you so fondly when we cherish our memories from the weekend. 

★★★★★

When we began our venue search, I had my heart set on an outdoor, garden-like ceremony. However, the moment we pulled up to High Pointe Estate, everything changed. We immediately knew this was where we wanted to get married, and it ended up being even more perfect than we could have imagined. The chapel at High Pointe Estate is absolutely breathtaking. The large windows flood the space with gorgeous natural light, creating an ethereal atmosphere that made me do a complete 180 on my outdoor ceremony plans. Not only is it visually stunning, but choosing the indoor chapel also eliminated the stress and uncertainty of unpredictable Texas weather—a decision we were so grateful for! The garden at High Pointe Estate was absolutely perfect for our first look. It provided a private, romantic setting with gorgeous natural beauty that made those intimate moments even more special. Our photographer captured incredible images against this beautiful backdrop. The outdoor space also provided the ideal backdrop for our cocktail hour. Our guests were able to mingle, enjoy drinks, and take in the beautiful views that High Pointe Estate has to offer. It gave everyone that outdoor garden experience I originally wanted, but in a perfectly curated way. Since we got married on a Saturday, we had access to the bungalow on the morning of our wedding. This was an incredible perk that provided so much extra space for me and my bridesmaids to get ready comfortably. Having a dedicated, spacious area for hair, makeup, and those special getting-ready moments made the morning feel relaxed and luxurious rather than cramped and chaotic. Having the ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception all in the same location was a game-changer for our guests. They were able to enjoy every moment of our wedding day to the fullest without worrying about transportation, timing, or missing any part of the celebration. The flow from one space to another was effortless. The reception space is truly grand and spacious, easily accommodating our guest count while still feeling intimate and special. The room has such incredible bones that it's beautiful on its own. High Pointe Estate offers white draping and romantic string lights as optional décor elements that rotate based on availability. We chose to leave it up to fate, and we were lucky enough to have both set up on our wedding day! The combination created an absolutely magical, dreamy atmosphere that our guests are still raving about!
