★★★★☆

I recently visited Mag’s, and it did not disappoint. The service was friendly and quick, and the atmosphere had that fun, local hangout vibe that makes you want to stay a while. They serve classic bar food, but don’t let that fool you—this place does it right. My personal favorite is the Philly Cheese Steak. It was packed with flavor and cooked perfectly. My son went with the chicken tenders and immediately said he loved them. My wife ordered the fish tacos; they were decent, though not her favorite of the night. And my daughter, who rarely finishes her meals, absolutely destroyed her All-American Burger—so that says a lot. Overall, Mag’s is a solid spot for comfort food done well. Definitely recommend giving it a try!
