★★★★★

My husband and I were looking for a new date night spot in Liberty Hill and decided to try Wish Kitchen + Bar — and we are SO glad we did. Everything about this place was amazing. The food was hands down some of the best we’ve had at a mom-and-pop restaurant. The drinks were incredible, and the overall vibe was super welcoming and cozy. We felt right at home which was so nice after a long week. My husband got the Hangover Burger, which came on an actual doughnut (yes… a doughnut ), and it was insanely good. I ordered the BLT, and it was fresh, flavorful, and honestly one of the best BLTs I’ve had. Shoutout to our server Bradley — he was awesome. Super friendly, cracking jokes with us, and always making sure we were good. The service really made the night even better. If you’re looking for a fun, relaxed date night spot with amazing food and great service, this place is a must. We will definitely be back with the family!

I had the Hangover Burger, Old Fashioned, and the Frozen Espresso Martini. The burger was surprisingly awesome. I never thought a donut bun on a burger with pepper jack cheese would be so good but they made this oddety a hit for me. The old fashioned was made with the love and more importantly with the Bulleit Rye that any expert would regard with favor. Finally, the Espresso Martini was like a milkshake for adults. I highly recommend this place. Also, the people are some of the greatest.
