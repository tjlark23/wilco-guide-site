About

All Care Therapies of Georgetown is a physical therapist in Georgetown, TX, offering physical therapist, occupational therapist, speech pathologist services. With a 4.8-star rating from 132 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated physical therapy providers in Williamson County.

Breanna Hawkins

★★★★★

My daughter has a neuromuscular disease and her doctors recommended therapy to help manage it. She has OT, PT, feeding, and speech therapy, so we spend a lot of time at All Care. One thing that's readily apparent is that all the therapists there are passionate about their work and go above and beyond for all their clients. My daughter's therapists, Jess, Gia, Patty and Kat, do an incredible job recognizing my daughter's needs and limitations and tailoring each of their sessions to her specifically. They make their sessions fun but also ensure she's working, too. One session my daughter was having a meltdown about bananas and Jess incorporated bananas into the entire hour-long session- they made banana art and played banana games and she came out happy and ready for her next session. As a parent of a child who struggles with transitions, I appreciate Jess' flexibility! My daughter absolutely adores all her therapists and really enjoys coming to therapy. We have noticed a huge improvement in her emotional regulation, her behavior, her physical abilities, her stamina, and her speech! We saw my sister for Thanksgiving and she was shocked that she could understand what my daughter was saying. The therapy is incredibly effective and we're so grateful for All Care. We highly recommend them to anyone in the Georgetown area!

★★★★★

I was diagnosed with Parkinson's Disease in 2018 and earlier this year my neurologist recommended All Care Therapies as a way to keep the flexibility and movement that PD takes away over time. I was assigned a dedicated Therapist (Callie) who specializes in PD treatment (specifically the PWR system) and she developed a program specifically for me and my PD related issues. Since working with Callie, my posture is much improved and my balance and flexibility have been noticeably stronger. In addition to medication; diet and exercise are vital in the management of PD. I cannot imagine a better partner than All Care therapies on my PD journey. Mike Gray
