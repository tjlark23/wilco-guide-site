★★★★★

Calvin and Scott were angels in disguise. My mother broke her hip and had hip replacement surgery. The hospital discharged her. They arranged for her to be transported by another medical transport company to a Rehab Center. The other companies drver threw her discharge paperwork at her. Then he left her suitcase on the hospital sidewalk and drove off. He was speeding and driving recklessly to the rehab center. When I arrived at the rehab center, my mother has in great pain. The rehab center was rundown. So, I decided that I would take my mother home. The rehab administrators were very helpful. They called Lone Star. As I was talking to Scott, he could hear my mother was in pain and not wanting to getback into another transport vehicle. He suggested that they use a Broda Chair transport. It's like a big lazy boy chair. His best driver, Calvin , was returning to end his shift. He said, he would ask him to make another run. Scott was concerned that we wouldn't have enough manpower to get her loaded into the chair. He decided that he would come with Calvin to help. Scott is the Executive Director of the company, no less. When they arrived, they explained to my mom; how they were going to put my mom in the Broda chair. She didn't want to change chairs because she was in so much pain. They allowed her to be in charge. If she was feeling any pain from the transfer. They would stop until she wanted to proceed. Once, she was in the chair and comfortable. Her personality changed, she was smiling, laughing and flirting with Calvin. On the ride home. Calvin would tell her about bumps in the road and curves. They had an excellent conversation. When they arrived at the house. They brought her into her room and got her into bed. My mom was in charge once again. They explained to her, how they were going to transfer her from the chair into the bed. It all went smoothly. They went above and beyond the service promised. It was a day from hell for me and my mother. But Calvin and Scott brought sunlight into a dark day; to the point of tears from me. I highly recommend this company. Its not about fitting in one more run. Its about taking care of their customers; but they treat you like family. God bless you, Calvin. God bless you, Scott. Sincerely, Lance Girard
