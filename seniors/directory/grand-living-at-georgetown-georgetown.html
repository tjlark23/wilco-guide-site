My wife and I recently visited Grand Living at Georgetown to spend time with our dear friends who recently moved into this community, and we were absolutely blown away by the experience. From the moment we arrived, we were welcomed with such genuine warmth by staff. It’s clear that the staff here truly care about the residents and their families. Abi truly stood out and left an incredible impression on both of us. From the very first moment we met her, it was clear she is someone special. She radiates warmth and passion for what she does. We had the pleasure of being invited to watch a fitness activity, and she went above and beyond to make us feel welcomed. Before the class, Abi noticed a subtle shift in a resident’s mood and quietly checked in with the resident privately. It was a small, thoughtful moment that reflects how staff pay attention to individual needs and personalize each resident’s daily experience, even when no one is watching. Throughout the class, that same personal connection continued to shine through. Abi’s ability to motivate through kindness was remarkable; always leading with positive reinforcement before offering gentle corrections, ensuring everyone felt supported and capable every step of the way. It brought such warmth to our hearts knowing that our friends have found a community like Grand Living, one that not only provides exceptional care but fosters belonging, purpose, and connection. Abi, along with the rest of the Grand Living team, exemplifies everything you would hope to find in a community like this. To Abi and the rest of the Grand Living team, thank you for what you do!
