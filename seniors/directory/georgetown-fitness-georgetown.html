About

Georgetown Fitness is a gym in Georgetown, TX, offering gym, fitness center, personal trainer services. With a 5-star rating from 1117 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated fitness & exercise providers in Williamson County.

★★★★★

I started training here in January 2023 after a triple heart bypass in August 2022 and follow on PT until the end of 2022. In early January 2023 my cardiologist was reviewing my case and he said “I want you to look like one of those ‘skinny fit’ old guys waiting to see me in my waiting room.” I told him the only thing that ever worked for me was weight training with a personal trainer. I had tried to find a good professional trainer in Georgetown, but the trainers I found were terrible. He said he knew a great one that was his personal trainer. He introduced me and he indeed was a true professional and exercise physiologist who knew the science of weight training. I worked with him about 15 months, when he had to leave for a job in another town. But he picked another trainer for me who he thought was excellent and I would like. His name is Michael Schwertner and he is fantastic. I have made excellent muscle gains with Michael. So since starting to do weight training at Georgetown Fitness with personal trainers, I have lost about 50+ pounds and gained a lot of muscle at the same time. I highly recommend Georgetown Fitness and their team of professional trainers. I’ve attached a before and after picture to show my results. I’ll be 77 in about 2 weeks. Train hard, eat right, and you can get great results too.

I am visiting from out of town, and this gym immediately made me feel welcome. The facility is clean, well organized, and clearly well cared for. The equipment selection is outstanding, with a wide variety that supports every style of training. As someone who takes bodybuilding very seriously, I was impressed by how well equipped the space is and how easy it is to get a complete, structured workout in. The Matrix machines are a personal favorite, and this gym has an excellent lineup of them. The atmosphere is both friendly and focused, making it a great fit for anyone, from beginners to serious lifters. Whether you are a dedicated bodybuilder, someone working on general fitness, visiting from out of town, or living in the area, this is a gym you will look forward to coming back to. I highly recommend it.
