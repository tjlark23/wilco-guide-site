About

Blue Corn Harvest Bar & Grill, Georgetown is a restaurant in Georgetown, TX, offering mexican restaurant, bar & grill, southern restaurant (us) services. With a 4.6-star rating from 3386 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated restaurants providers in Williamson County.

★★★★☆

This Blue Corn harvest Bar and Grill is located W 7th Street just off the main downtown square in Georgetown. There is free parking around the restaurant, opening hours are 11am - 10pm (weekdays) and 10am to 10pm (weekends). There is a nice indoor seating area and menu features a nice selection of home-cooked, traditional Texas and Tex-Mex dishes. Had an early lunch here recently, found the Chicken fried chicken lunch to be very good. Chicken was nicely crispy fried yet still succulent and flavourful. Mash and gravy tasted perfect, as did the grilled vegetables. This was a very good lunch, good portion size and rightly priced as well. Would gladly return for another meal at Blue Corn Harvest based on this recent experience.
