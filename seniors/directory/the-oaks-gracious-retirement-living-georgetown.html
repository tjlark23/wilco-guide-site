Virginia Livesay
Oct 12, 2025
★★★★★

I lived at the Oaks for 1 year and 5 months. I moved there because I was a caregiver for someone who had become difficult for me to care for. And while it is an independent living situation, the 3 meals a day took the burden of cooking off my shoulders. I met the loveliest people there. They were friendly and kind. They emphasized Community and everyone there looked out for each other in a respectful and private way. The building itself was beautifully kept, decorated tastefully, clean, and comfortable with many places to sit and read, visit, and play games. The grounds were also well planned with safe places to walk and play with your dog. The prices are more reasonable than others in town, and there are no contracts. Each person is offered a safety pendent, worn around the neck, which can be pushed in case of a fall. It is a great place to live and I highly recommend it.
