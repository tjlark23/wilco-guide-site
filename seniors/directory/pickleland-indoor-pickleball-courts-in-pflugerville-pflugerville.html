About

Pickleland - Indoor Pickleball Courts in Pflugerville is a attractions in Pflugerville, TX, offering pickleball court, event venue, fitness center services. With a 5-star rating from 808 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated pickleball courts providers in Williamson County.

Visited Pickleland for my first time to take a clinic with professional pickleball player Jack Munro. The clinic was the best one I have ever attended. The facility was amazing. The staff were attentive, polite, and knowledgeable. It really felt like a community there. I highly recommend it. They have a great schedule of open play, drills, clinics and more for novices to DUPR ranked players.

Pickleland has a great, welcoming vibe with super friendly staff and well-maintained courts. They host great events, which add a lot to the experience. Fair warning—it can get a little smelly at times 🤣, but overall it’s a fun and solid place to play pickleball.
