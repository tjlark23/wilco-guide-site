About

Willie's Grill & Icehouse is a restaurant in Georgetown, TX, offering american restaurant, bar & grill, chicken restaurant services. With a 4.5-star rating from 3519 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated restaurants providers in Williamson County.

Willie’s Restaurant absolutely crushed it. From the second we walked in, the vibe was warm and welcoming, the kind of place that makes you instantly feel like you picked the right spot.
