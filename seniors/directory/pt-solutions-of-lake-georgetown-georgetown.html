★★★★★

What an incredible staff and environment! I can’t give PT Solutions enough praise. I started going to PT on Phase two of my Physical Therapy training at the beginning of the year after having undergone spinal cord surgery in mid November. When I started with them, I was walking at careful pace, I wasn’t bending or twisting at all. I was on a five pound lifting limit. By the end of two weeks, I was not only bending, but using a 20lb ball to the floor and back repetitively. My walking pace was back to normal and my pain had gone from a 4-6 down to a 1-2. I primarily worked with Matt, who was Awesome. He pushed me and encouraged me, but never forced me, he always made sure to ask and really listen to me about how my body was feeling and responding. I worked with a couple of the other trainers, but I’m so sorry guys I can’t remember your names, but all of them were excellent. The Tech Assistant’s name is Christopher, and he was always helpful and attentive as well. Trust me, If you’re looking for a Physical Therapist, do yourself a favor, start your search hear and let it end here, you will be in the best of hands. You won’t regret it!
