Crunch Fitness - Round Rock is a gym in Round Rock, TX, offering gym, fitness center, personal trainer services. With a 4.5-star rating from 1165 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated fitness & exercise providers in Williamson County.

I also want to highlight my trainer, Jaidon — he’s been an incredible part of my fitness journey. He’s professional, knowledgeable, and genuinely cares about helping me reach my goals. I’ve seen real progress and feel stronger every week thanks to his guidance and encouragement. If you’re looking for a supportive place to train with great childcare and an amazing trainer like Jaidon, this is definitely the gym to join!
