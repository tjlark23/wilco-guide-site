About

Platinum Resort At Berry Springs is a assisted living facility in Georgetown, TX, offering assisted living facility services. With a 5-star rating from 23 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated senior living communities providers in Williamson County.

★★★★★

Berry Springs Platinum Resort Assisted Living truly lives up to its name — it’s a place where compassion, comfort, and quality care come together beautifully. From the moment you walk in, the environment feels warm, inviting, and full of life. The staff go above and beyond to ensure every resident feels valued, respected, and cared for like family. The facility itself is clean, modern, and thoughtfully designed, with plenty of cozy spaces and engaging activities that make each day enjoyable. The management team is attentive and professional, always keeping families informed and involved. You can tell they genuinely care about the residents’ well-being and happiness. Whether it’s the friendly caregivers, the delicious meals, or the peaceful atmosphere, Berry Springs sets the standard for what assisted living should be. I would highly recommend this community to anyone looking for a safe, supportive, and truly loving home for their loved ones.
