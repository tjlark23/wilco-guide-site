I was looking to find the perfect place for my mom, she had been in the hospital and needed more care than I could provide. I toured several options a while back, none of them were perfect. While the home was reasonably clean, nicely furnished, and appeared to be run well, I did not pick this homely option. The atmosphere felt a bit disingenuous. While I typically support small businesses, I was unable to warm up to this humble little place. I am certain the rustic domestic environment is endearing to some, it was just not what I was looking for, for my mom.
