★★★★★

He was honestly a little hesitant to write this review because I don’t want you going there… I don’t want this place to be too busy for whenever I plan to stop by… 🙋🏻‍♀️ Bill, the owner, already told me that he sells out of lasagna every night and I want to make sure there’s some left for me!🤣 So for anybody that drives into Round Rock to get a good Chicago dog, now you don’t have to because they have come to Georgetown! 😍 I love the ambience of this place and the server was super sweet. It was lunch so I did a half sandwich and half salad (I’m not sure how that salad counts as a half, it was big) and we also started with the fresh tomato basil soup. 🥣😋 I know my husband is going to be super excited to hear that the “Chicago dog place” that we used to have to drive almost 30 minutes to get to is now right around the corner! And they do a fabulous expanded menu for dinner. All kidding aside I highly recommend this place Dinner was great! The lasagne was as promised - huge

★★★☆☆

Definitely 5 stars for effort. Really great ambiance. Service is great. Not a complaint, but the lunch menu was disappointing. If you’re not up for a sandwich, then the lunch menu is anemic. The dinner menu is more weighted to pizza and pasta. Just wish the lunch menu offered more than hot dogs, sandwiches and spaghetti. Where’s the deep dish? Where’s the muffuletta? Calzone? Pizza- there are literally 5 places with awesome thin crust pizzas on the square. Bring the deep dish. Nobody has the deep dish except via 313. Anyway. A for effort. C- for creativity. Secret: we’re looking for things nobody else offers.
