About

Fonda Marin Mexican Restaurant is a restaurant in Georgetown, TX, offering mexican restaurant services. With a 4.9-star rating from 39 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated restaurants providers in Williamson County.

★★★★★

This is hands down the most authentic place in Georgetown I've found. It's family owned and operated which shows tremendously in their food. Prices are fair and the ownership is amazing. I typically don't write home about a breakfast taco but these people surpassed my expectations. Take note that their homemade corn tortillas and salsa caught my attention which is a great foundation to this style of cuisine. They have a new regular for sure! I love Fonda Marin!

★★★★☆

1/2026 Don't let the location inside a convenience store fool you - this kitchen has some legit Mexican dishes. Among the most popular they say are the Falutas, which were sold out by 6pm on my Friday evening visit. The fajitas grilled up fresh on order were delicious. They have a nice selection of hot sauces ranging from "f-yeah it's hot" to "holy cow I think I'm on fire" but those are optional and the dishes are moderately spicy, perfect for most people's tastes. There are several tables for indoor dining, or grab a to-go to take with you.

★★★★★

This restaurant in the back of a gas station convenience store is the best Mexican food to be found in Georgetown. I had the cheese empanadas and chicken tacos. My husband has chicken enchiladas. The food was cooked to order, fresh and delicious. It was served with a flight of salas and warm tortillas. Drinks are an assortment of Mexican sodas, ice tea, lemonade and water. The place has a friendly taco truck vibe. Our server was very sweet and attentive. The only thing missing is margaritas. We will definitely be back. Thanks Fonda de Marin for the good food!
