If I could give 10 stars I would!! University Village Memory Care is truly the best memory care community. The staff—especially Terra, Donnetta, Dorie, and the caregivers—are some of the kindest, most compassionate people I have ever met. They are incredibly patient with my mom and took the time to truly get to know her as a person, not just a resident. I live far south and drive over an hour to visit, and I do so with complete peace of mind knowing my mom is genuinely well cared for. She is safe, supported, and—most importantly—actually enjoying her time there. That alone means everything to our family. If you are looking for a memory care community where your loved one will be treated with dignity, kindness, and genuine love, I cannot recommend University Village Memory Care enough.

University Village is truly the best of the best! The staff are patient and kind with both residents and family members. The care coordinators and many of the staff have been there for years. The most amazing thing is how happy most of the staff are! I mean, their job is one of the hardest on the planet - caring for people whose brains are being ripped apart by disease and who can no longer communicate with the rest of us in a normal way. And yet, many of the staff members go through their whole day with a smile on their face and love in their hearts - it is truly amazing! Thank you, Terra, Dorie, Donnetta, Abby, Mardi, Craig, and all the staff, for everything you have done and everything you continue to do! We deeply appreciate you.

Peggy Wilson
Sep 8, 2019
★★★★★

Terra and all of the staff at University Village are incredible. We are so grateful we had our Mom there, and even more grateful for the care provided. The facility is small, so the ratio of staff to residents is really good. In addition, there is an onsite Hospice and Home Health facility that was able to complete my Mom's care until she passed away. Terra focuses on her staff and does everything she can to keep them happy working there. Many have been there for years. Also, you get to know the caregivers well. She has two resident care managers, that are always available, and extremely responsive to any questions or updates. They are happy to communicate by text, email or phone. The facility has alot of natural light and open areas. The kitchen is like one in a lovely home. They even have a labradoodle named Sophie that lives there and visits every so often. I highly recommend University Village Memory Care and Assisted Living for your loved one.

★★★★★

I want to publicly commend University Village Memory Care for the excellent care for nearly 9 years for my brother Dale Cohrs. From all the parts of my brother’s care over those years he had beyond above average care from all the loving folks that have worked there over the years. I don't know how they find their employees but whatever method they use is has to be very thorough to have such great staff. I have visited many care facilities over the years and never have I seen such loving and caring folks all in one place. With many folks with mental and physical afflictions of all sorts they handle it well. The atmosphere in the place in my opinion is uplifting to a patient in their conditions. I always felt at home with all that worked there! They love to see family involved with the persons they care for. Of course with humans there can be a problem crop up but if that ever happens they are quick to act, they listen to family concerns, and the patient is at their upmost importance. I doubt that there is another facility like this on in the area. I'm saying you will have to look long and hard to find any better facility to care for you loved one!!! My brother was buried today from complications of Alzheimer's which had suffer may years with along with a stroke that disabled him at the early stages of Alzheimer. I thank everyone at this facility for the excellent care and love you bestowed on my brother and my family members. Larry Cohrs
