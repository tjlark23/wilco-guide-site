About

Vets Park Pickleball Courts is a attractions in Cedar Park, TX, offering park services. With a 4.6-star rating from 47 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated pickleball courts providers in Williamson County.

Aug 9, 2024
★★★★☆

Great courts, they are really close together so if it’s busy there’s a lot of balls crossing into your court during game play. There’s also some gaps in the fence but there’s easy access around the court to retrieve balls. Lighting is pretty good. Fencing is sturdy, there’s new concrete and benches but no shade yet. Looks like some is coming though. There were a lot of people out for a Thursday evening. Lights go out right at 10pm.
