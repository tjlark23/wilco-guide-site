★★★★★

Since Cecilia Garza, who goes by Cece, became the new director, the change at this memory care facility has been remarkable. She has truly turned the place around — from hiring a new cook to addressing staffing issues and holding people accountable. The facility and individual rooms are now consistently clean, the food is warm and good, and there is a noticeable sense of care and liveliness throughout the building. Most importantly, the residents are well cared for, and you can feel the difference in the atmosphere. I’m incredibly grateful for the improvements and the leadership Cece has brought.

The decision to place my family member in someone else’s care was a difficult one. The moment I met with Kiley, I was comfortable with my decision. The facility was clean, open floor plan , and the employees all had smiles. They quickly got to know my family member and took the time to account for his preferences, likes and dislikes. Spoke to him by name, always treating him with respect. Of the other facilities I visited, Avalon is hands down the best!

Mom was in the best care I could have ever imagined at Avalon Memory Care. I found this place to be my second home. The time and attention the staff gave mom was above and beyond just their job. They loved her like their own mother and made her feel like the queen she was. Thank you Avalon. Nov 14, 2025
★☆☆☆☆

At first we blamed delusions. On days he wouldn’t wake up, or his speech was unusually slurred, we blamed the dementia. But the family began to notice more: his clothes were filthy, his hearing aids were never charged. Injuries were “falls”. His toenails were so neglected that we feared he could lose at least one toe. We moved him. The first week, he continually asked new caregivers, “Are you going to hurt me? Hit me? Pinch me? Push me?” Today, tho the dementia continues to steal his mind, he smiles. He’s relaxed. He’s so much better! BEWARE of Avalon!

★★★★★

Our family is deeply grateful for the care and compassion shown by Avalon to my great uncle during his time there. From the moment he arrived, it was clear that this facility was more than just a place—it was a community of caring individuals dedicated to their residents' well-being. The staff treated my great uncle with dignity and kindness, ensuring he was comfortable, engaged, and well cared for until the very end. Their understanding of memory care and their patience with residents and families alike truly stood out. They not only supported him but also provided guidance and reassurance to us during a difficult time. I would wholeheartedly recommend Avalon to anyone searching for a memory care facility for their loved one. Their commitment to excellence and genuine compassion makes all the difference. Thank you, Avalon, for everything you did for my great uncle and our family.
