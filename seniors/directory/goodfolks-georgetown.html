★★★★☆

We got to try Goodfolks a few weeks ago for the first time! We came on a Thursday evening around 5:30, and there was no wait. The atmosphere and vibes of the restaurant are very cute! However not every dish that we got was great. One of the things they seem to be known for are their biscuits, and I was excited to try them, but was left disappointed. They were very dry and hard. We also got the pimento cheese and crackers as a starter. It was basically cheese and crackers slapped onto a plate, with no flavor or added things to make it special. The chicken sandwich that I got was great, however! It had a super thick piece of chicken and had great flavor. I would come back to get this again, but not the other items!

M
Marsha Russell
Oct 22, 2025
★★★★★

Love this place! We had a terrific experience here when we came for lunch on a Thursday. The space — a lovingly preserved corner of the 1896 Lockett Building on Georgetown’s square — is inviting, bright and airy, with a central room surrounded by a wraparound balcony. Our server greeted us warmly and brought our drinks promptly. The full bar has a nice choice of craft beers on tap, cocktails, and wine. The menu also has lots of enticing choices; so many things sounded good! We ordered the chicken salad sandwich with sweet potato fries and the Fried Chicken Salad (pieces of fried chicken breast on mixed greens with bits of bacon and blue cheese crumbles), and both were absolutely delicious! We couldn’t resist the dessert special, Strawberry Buttermilk Cake with fresh berries, crème anglaise, and ice cream, and oh my goodness gracious, it was divine! We told the server it needs to be on the regular menu and she said that’s the plan. From the lunch crowd that was there that day, this is a very popular place, and based on our experience, it certainly merits that. We will be back again and again.

Dipping sauces were SO good and yes, we ordered pretty much every sauce available. Shanna was also delightfully funny and kind. We had a great time today.
