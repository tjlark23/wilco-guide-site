I highly recommend FYZICAL to anyone looking for PT, especially athletes. Kylie guided me through rehab for both a shoulder sprain and an ankle sprain during my competitive roller derby season, ensuring I was able to compete in two tournaments despite a tight turnaround from injury. Even when I sustained a secondary ankle sprain mid-recovery, I was still able to go into tournaments feeling strong, stable, and confident thanks to her preparation. In addition, their office staff was incredibly helpful from my first phone call, walking me through insurance, explaining my coverage, and outlining costs before I even scheduled an appointment. Throughout my treatment, they made the administrative side of things a breeze. I am so grateful for the care I received and would absolutely return for any future PT needs.
