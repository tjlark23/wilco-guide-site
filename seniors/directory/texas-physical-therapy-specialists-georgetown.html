Texas Physical Therapy Specialists is a physical therapy clinic in Georgetown, TX, offering physical therapy clinic, physical therapist, sports medicine clinic services. With a 4.9-star rating from 234 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated physical therapy providers in Williamson County.

I've recently finished my course of treatment at the facility in Georgetown. The facility was well maintained and the staff there are excellent - friendly and helpful- from the front office folks to the PT techs and the PTs themselves. I especially enjoyed working with Dr. Trevis Green there. From the initial assessment to each of the treatment sessions, Trevis was great about listening to my feedback on whether a particular treatment was working or not. Based on that, he'll adjust the intensity or recommend other treatments. I credit his ability to work with his patient as a team to pinpoint the issue and prescribe the right set of treatments to address the root issues. Highly recommend working with Dr. Trevis.
