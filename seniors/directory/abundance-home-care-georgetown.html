We used Abundance Home Care back in October 2023. Stephanie W, the owner, came over to our house and took the time to get to know us and our loved one that needed care. I have to say, Stephanie and her team are very courteous, kind, compassionate, professional, and really care about their client as if it were their own loved one. We needed a couple of shifts for our loved one, and the team worked with us to find the best caregiver for him, and were able to accommodate our weird shift schedule. I have recommended Stephanie's team to several people that I have met who have inquired about searching for caregivers, and I will continue to refer/recommend Abundance Home Care to anyone who is needing a caregiver. I was very impressed with their mission statement, and the type of care provided aligns across the board with their mission and values. Our loved one was very happy with the caregivers that were assigned by Abundance Home Care, and we were always in the loop about the care because there was a daily log that was kept of the routine and everything that went on with the care. I would highly recommend setting an appointment with them and getting to know Stephanie and how she can help you provide the best care for your loved one.
