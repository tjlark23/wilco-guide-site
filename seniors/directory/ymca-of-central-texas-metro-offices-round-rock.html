About

YMCA of Central Texas - Metro Offices is a non-profit organization in Round Rock, TX, offering non-profit organization services. With a 4.5-star rating from 161 reviews, they are one of the highest-rated fitness & exercise providers in Williamson County.

Just started my membership after enjoying their 3-day pass and being impressed with their recent renovations. I was a member back around 2018(?) and the bldg felt outdated. It wasn't horrible before but, the upgrades were definitely welcome. My only complaint (?) is that there used to be a back entrance which is now an employees-only door. It was nice being able to park from that side since I live in east RR and come to the facility from that direction. Having a secondary entrance would allow people more parking options besides main entrance and help with foot traffic but, I am merely suggesting as it was convenient parking on that side and they may not have adequate staffing for additional entrances. I like the new layout and design; it feels much more planned out, spacious and designed for specific uses vs feeling a bit cramped before - it feels so fancy!
