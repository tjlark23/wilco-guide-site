About

Hippo Social Performance Club is a gym in Hutto, TX, offering gym, community health center, fitness center services. Rated 4.9 stars based on 17 reviews, they provide quality fitness & exercise for Williamson County seniors.

This place is such a gem!! It has all the amenities that make for a great escape: sauna, cold plunge, running trails, workout classes, wellness groups, etc. Many of the planned activities take place outside. (There’s nothing better than lifting weights while watching the sun set.) MOST IMPORTANTLY: the people that run Hippo Social Club are so caring and passionate about helping others into a healthy lifestyle. They foster an environment where people are encouraged to get to know each other genuinely and personally. Living in the suburbs, I often find myself daydreaming about how much more socially fulfilled I’d be if I lived in a close-knit village instead. I think Hippo Social Club brings me as close to that daydream as physically possible. Hippo Social Club fosters a close-knit community of people passionate about health+wellness, connecting with others, and the outdoors. The best part is they’re ALWAYS open to meeting new people and I never once felt left-out or excluded as a new member. :)
