★★★★★

We've been going here for a few years now. I can't tell you how nervous I was for my first visit, as it had been roughly 20 years since I'd seen a dentist. (Lack of insurance.) I was certain they were going to find multiple cavities and suggest tons of work. To my utter shock, I still didn't have a single cavity! Gingivitis and mild gum disease, sure, but there was never anything recommended that I felt was unnecessary. A few months ago, I had a random tooth bleed after sucking on a sucker. Dr. Mattias had Dr. Heinz come to do a consult with me. After looking at it from a number of different angles and consulting previous dental records, he made the decision that I didn't need that tooth removed. Another score for them! If that wasn't all, I feel like the front desk staff are truly the best. They take time to explain things. I'm so happy to have most of my family going here! (They only take 15 & up, understandable. They aren't outfitted for pediatric dentistry, which is totally fine).
