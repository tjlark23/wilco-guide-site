★★★★★

I hope my review helps guide you in finding the very best memory care / senior living home for your loved one. After 2 long weeks of touring multiple memory care locations in the Georgetown and the surrounding area, we chose the Delaney and here’s why. First, my high school classmate from 1987 actually worked for The Delaney for 8 years so I had some good inside information as to why it’s ranked as the best facility in the area. She ended up putting own her mom there so that in itself gave me even more confidence in what can be one of the hardest decisions you will make in your life. Our parents raised us and took care of us the best they could so now it was my time to give back and find my mom the best place to now call home. It is a very clean place and homey environment atmosphere. So the results are in; after 2 months and 14 days. I absolutely made the right decision for my mom. Shannan the memory care director has a wonderful dedicated loving team who will be completely focused and care for your loved one in every way possible. Shannan is just one call away from allowing me to check on my mom when I’m out of town. She’s has even taken my calls on her day off. This is an example of the high level of service and response the Delaney gives their residents and the families. They make her bed everyday, they help her shower, they help her in the bathroom and even the cleaning staff (Tiffany) who keeps the place extremely clean loves and cares for my mom. They have med techs whose sole purpose is to administer medications on time, every time. They have nurses on site 24 hours a day and I’m one text away from requesting anything my mom may need or want. They are also a class B facility which is very important to know. Just so many care givers dedicated to your loved ones needs. It’s always enjoyable to visit my mom and take her to lunch or dinner in the many dining rooms they have. The food is amazing and they have 2 executive chef’s preparing some really great dishes. I would also point out that I have met the director of maintenance which is responsible for keeping the place so clean and everything working and they do a fine job. They even have a wonderful activity director coordinating environment and fun for the residents. Go visit the Delaney and ask to meet the directors and you will understand how they have earned my perfect review. Remember, you are doing what you have to do in a tough situation and knowing your loved one is safe and cared for so well will bring you comfort and peace of mind. If your loved one can’t be home anymore, this is the next best place to call home. Blessings on your journey. Derek
